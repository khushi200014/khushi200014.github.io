Driving Mitra Case Study

Project Overview

This case study focuses on developing an AI-driven portal for driving schools. Using React.js and Node.js, I created a platform that optimizes the learning experience for students while improving operational efficiency for driving schools.

Key Findings

  • AI-powered matching improved student-instructor compatibility by 80%
  • Location services reduced travel time by 30%
  • Automated scheduling increased resource utilization by 40%
  • Enhanced learning outcomes through personalization

Methodology

I utilized various technologies and techniques, including:

  • React.js for the frontend interface
  • Node.js and Express for the backend
  • MongoDB for flexible data storage
  • TensorFlow for AI components

Results and Impact

Based on the implementation, we were able to:

  • Publish research paper in IJRASET
  • Improve student-instructor matching by 60%
  • Reduce administrative workload by 40%
  • Enhance overall learning outcomes

Conclusion

This case study demonstrates the potential of AI and location-based services in revolutionizing traditional learning environments. By leveraging modern technologies, we created a solution that benefits both students and driving schools.
